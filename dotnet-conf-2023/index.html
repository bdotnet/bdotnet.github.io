About The Conference

.NET Conf 2023 - Recap Bengaluru is a free, developer conference organized by the Bangalore DotNet UG (BDotNet), Dot Net Learners House community and Microsoft. Come and Join us to learn about the latest and greatest of Microsoft Technologies directly from Microsoft folks, Microsoft MVPs, Industry experts, and Community rockstars.
